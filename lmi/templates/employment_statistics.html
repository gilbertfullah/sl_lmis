Figure: Literacy Rates of Population 10 years and above

At the Regional level, the literacy rates are: Eastern Region – 47.5 percent; Northern Region 42.2 percent; Southern Region - 45.2 percent and Western Area - 74.5 percent.

Economic characteristics

The results show that 68.6 percent of the working age population (15-64 years) are economically active, known also as the labour force (employed and unemployed), and 31.4 percent are non-economically active.

Employment Rate

Figure above shows that 91.4 percent of the labour force are employed, and 8.6 percent are unemployed. The employment rate is almost the same for male and female, 91.5 percent and 91.3 percent, respectively. At the regional level, the employment rates are: Eastern Region - 94.1 percent, Northern Region - 93.5 percent, Southern Region - 92.1 percent and Western Area – 84.7 percent.
